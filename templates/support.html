Help & Support Center

Find answers to frequently asked questions below.

How do I borrow a book?

To borrow a book, you must first log in to your User account. Navigate to the "Browse Books" page, select a book that is marked as "Available," and click the "Borrow Book" button.

What is the difference between an Admin and a User?

Users can search our catalog, view book details, and borrow available books. Admins are responsible for managing the library; they can add new books to the system, edit existing book details, and delete books.

How can I see which books I have borrowed?

Once logged in, open the "User Menu" in the navigation bar and click on "My Borrowed Books" to see a complete list of your current rentals.

Why is the "Borrow Book" button disabled?

If a button is disabled and greyed out, it means another user has already borrowed that specific book. Check back later to see if its status has changed to "Available".
